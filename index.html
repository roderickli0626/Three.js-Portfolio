Seismic Tension

An engaging exhibit about seismologic research.

Challenge

In my Bachelor Thesis, I collaborated with seismologists from the Future Ocean Cluster to present their field of research in an exhibition situation. The goal was to convey their work to the public in an engaging way.

Result

The result is an exhibit showing live seismic activity in the Los Angeles Basin, inspired by old-fashioned seismographs. By interpreting live data from an online database, earthquakes can be displayed on a three-dimensional model of the area. If high seismic activity occurs, the surface of the object begins to move accordingly and the magnitude is displayed in a graphical representation. In this way, viewers can monitor the seismic activity and are given an understanding of the scientific research work.

Nominated for the Muthesius Award 2018

Collaborator: Future Ocean Cluster

Supervisor: Prof. Tom Duscher

My Role: Concept, Design, Implementation

Thank You for Applying

A critical look at AI hiring systems.

With the rise of Artificial Intelligence in recruitment, more and more job applications are evaluated by automated systems.

Challenge

Job applicants who are directly impacted by AI hiring decisions do not get to understand, question or contest the results. How might we enable understanding and facilitate a more critical way of engaging with AI hiring systems?

Result

With a range of digital interventions, we showcase how different areas of the current hiring process could be explained or disrupted. Thereby, we aim to spark discourse and enable critical thinking about the subject. Feedback Report - Rejected job seekers are often left without any explanations. The feedback report gives applicants an insight how the automated hiring decision was made.CHECKMATE - A web service that explains how hiring algorithms evaluate resumes. Additionally, it helps job seekers to improve their applications accordingly.​​​​​​​Incognito CV - This CV creator aims to enable a fairer hiring process. Could we avoid algorithmic discrimination by hiding proxy words that would reveal our gender, age or social status?

Team: Joshua von Hofen, Bedannita Mukherjee

My Role: Concept, UX/UI, Prototyping, Coding

SYNG Karaoke

A new karaoke system for the Norwegian franchise.

The karaoke bar franchise SYNG is all about having a fun night out. Unfortunately, their outdated karaoke system was not contributing to that. The goal for their new custom system was to be playful, unconventional but still so intuitive that even tipsy karaoke singers would have no problems using it.

Result

The new system covers all touch points necessary for a great karaoke experience. A back-end solution for admins to add new songs the music library, a booking system for SYNG’s staff and lastly, a new and improved interface for the customers to find and play their favorite songs.All touch points feature the bold and whacky look that makes SYNG so charming. The new touch screens enable customers not only to search for songs, but also explore categories and curated playlists. The system features audio-reactive animations and the increased button sizes also allow tipsy singers to find a song.Winner of Gulltaggen Award for 'Best Product and Service 2022'

Client: SYNG

Development: TRY Dig

Rubiks Festival

A streaming platform with festival vibes.

Sopra Steria is hosting an annual conference for its employees. In 2021, the typically physical event had to be digital. But since Sopra Steria's employees were already spending a lot of time in digital meetings, they did not want an experience that feels like yet another video conference. Consequently, our challenge was to create a streaming platform that gives you a festival feeling!

Result

We created platform hosted over a 100 talks with 2000 employees watching, reacting and connecting with their colleagues.A festival map served as the main navigation.Users were able to create and dress up an avatar.By entering one of the stages, users could follow a live stream with the ability to comment and react. To take a break from the live program, users could set up tents and invite their colleagues to hang out.

Winner of Magnet Award 2021 for 'Best campaign for own employees'

Client: Sopra Steria

Video production: Gyro

Development: TRY Pearl
